# Project Story

Consumers often purchase skincare products based on marketing, trends, influencers, or celebrity endorsements without fully understanding the ingredient list.

Many users struggle to determine whether a product contains potentially irritating ingredients or whether it is suitable for their skin type.

This project was developed to simplify skincare decision-making.

The AI Clean Beauty Recommender analyzes product ingredients, calculates cleanliness scores, identifies potential irritants, and recommends products based on skin type and skincare concerns.

The goal is to provide consumers with transparent, data-driven skincare recommendations that prioritize product safety and personalization.
